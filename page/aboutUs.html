About Batsumi Lodge

Discover our story, mission, and the team behind your exceptional stay

Our Story

Batsumi Lodge was born from a dream to create a haven of luxury and comfort in the heart of Galeshewe. Founded in 2010 by the Batsumi family, our guesthouse has grown from a small, family-run establishment into a beacon of hospitality in the Northern Cape.

Our journey began with a passion for sharing the beauty and culture of Galeshewe with visitors from around the world. Over the years, we've continuously evolved, always striving to enhance our guests' experiences while staying true to our roots and the warm spirit of South African hospitality.

Our Mission

At Batsumi Lodge, our mission is to provide an unparalleled luxury experience that celebrates the rich culture and natural beauty of Galeshewe. We are committed to:

  • Delivering exceptional service that exceeds our guests' expectations
  • Creating a warm, welcoming environment that feels like a home away from home
  • Promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit our local community
  • Showcasing the best of South African hospitality and cuisine
  • Continuously improving and innovating to enhance our guests' experiences
